Dragon Learning Center is a mobile application for scheduling tutoring sessions in specific subjects, designed for USFQ students. Users can sign up as either tutors or students, browse availability, and book appointments easily.

Tech stack: Angular, Ionic Framework, TypeScript, SCSS, JavaScript, HTML5, Firebase (backend), Figma and Adobe Illustrator (design & prototyping).
