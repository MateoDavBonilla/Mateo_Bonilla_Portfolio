Fragment is a metroidvania inspired by the theme of schizophrenia. You progress to uncover the story while interacting with various characters who challenge your identity and reveal who you may truly be.

Objetivos del proyecto

Proposal

  • Raise awareness about a taboo illness.
  • Develop a project that brings visibility to the condition.
  • Promote understanding that in some cases, people can live a normal life.

Research

  • Identify knowledge barriers about the illness.
  • Understand the level of awareness in specific cases.
  • Explore how much people know about mental health conditions.

Product

  • Design a video game that raises awareness about mental health disorders.
  • Create gameplay focused on the illness as the main character.
  • Combine awareness, visibility, and entertainment in a single product.

Definition

The Project

  • The main objective is to distinguish what is truly real.

Theme and Story

  • The main theme is the internal acceptance of schizophrenia and the search for mental balance.
  • The world represents how the main character perceives reality.

Results

A digital experience that allows anyone to become aware of these types of mental health conditions, learn, explore, and engage without intimidation. The game integrates mechanics, gradual education, and visibility to present these topics in an accessible, engaging, and entertaining way.

  • Complete demo of the first level of the game, including gameplay, interaction, and story.
  • Integrated narrative within secondary NPC characters.
  • Real mechanics inspired by actual mental health conditions.

Challenges and Learnings

This project was both rewarding and challenging. Throughout the process, it was fulfilling to see how an initial idea gradually took shape and evolved with each iteration. Observing how research, design, and development came together to create a real solution helped me strengthen my skills, apply empathy and resilience, and trust in the creative process.

  • Working with mental health topics, although complex, can lead to interesting design ideas.
  • Implementing mental conditions into gameplay mechanics was challenging but very enjoyable.
  • I learned how to translate concepts and designs into functional code.

Next Steps

  • Develop a full game exploring these topics in more depth.
  • Release the game on platforms such as Steam or Play Store.
  • Build a community to increase awareness and visibility of projects like this.
