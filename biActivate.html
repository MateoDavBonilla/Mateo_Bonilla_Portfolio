CLIENT

BI Activate is a website for a banking organization designed to make collaboration easier across teams. It helps employees discover peer-led initiatives (clubs, activities, and projects), connect with each other, and join effortlessly from any area of the company.

The site is hosted on Firebase and built using HTML5, SCSS, and JavaScript. Design and prototypes were created using Figma, and promotional videos were produced in Adobe After Effects.

Challenges and Learnings

Developing a platform that felt both corporate and human-centered was a key challenge. Through this project I learned how to align UX/UI principles with institutional identity while maintaining an inviting tone that promotes collaboration.

  • Balancing accessibility and visual identity for a banking brand.
  • Testing with real users from different departments improved adoption insights.
  • Managing deployment and maintenance through Firebase strengthened my full-stack workflow.
