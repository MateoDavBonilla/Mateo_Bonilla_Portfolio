BI Activate is a website for a banking organization designed to make collaboration easier across teams. It helps employees discover peer-led initiatives (clubs, activities, and projects), connect with each other, and join effortlessly from any area of the company. The site is publicly hosted on Firebase Hosting.

Tech stack: HTML5, SCSS, JavaScript; Firebase (Hosting); Figma (UI/UX design & prototyping); Adobe After Effects (video editing).
